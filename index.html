ABOUT TANTRAFIESTA
Tantra-fiesta is a premier annual technical event organized by IIITN. Our event promotes research, problem-solving skills, and fostering a sense of community among tech-enthusiasts. The theme of 5th edition of Tantra-fiesta’23 is “Genesis unleashed: AI dawn” focused on exploring the dynamics of deep learning, automation, Neural networks and inspiring students to contribute in the latest advancements in artificial intelligence. We unite visionaries, coders, entrepreneurs, animators, web-app developers, designers, and gamers to showcase their talents and embark on a journey towards growth and achievement. Last year, we witnessed a surge of over 1,000 participants from across India, with an impressive turnout of over 10,000 attendees at our event. Our technical fest showcased cutting-edge technology, innovative ideas, and a plethora of engaging fun activities to captivate our audience. This year we have an exciting array of activities lined up for our tech-fest, including coding contests, design-a-thons, Robo-Rumble, Capture the Flag, and much more
